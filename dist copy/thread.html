Fashionista

A famous online fashion retailer in the UK wanted to perfect their post purchase journey and come up with recommendations for improvements in their overall experience.

Problem

The retailer knew that there are some problems that led the customers to rate their service with bad reviews. They didn’t knew where and how things were going wrong. They also didn’t knew where to start and which problem to prioritise.

Process

Talking with customers from the target group of the product and mapping their post purchase emotional journey to find out where things were actually going wrong and come up with ideas to improve the experience.

Emotional Journey Map

The emotional journey map helped us to identify the problems faced by the customers and their severity.

Problem prioritisation & Ideation

Going through the process of design thinking to defining the problems, prioritising them and then to come up with ideas to solve them.

Recommendations

Chilled out deliveries:

Not all deliveries need to be express deliveries. Incentivise the customers who opt for relaxed deliveries, but keep up the promise.

Shop around the corner:

Make returns and replacements as easy as going to a shop around the corner. Try some innovative ways here.

Insta Store credits:

Introduce customers to the concept of liquidity by giving them instant credits to shop with, on placing the returns.
